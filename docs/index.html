tinker.

"for the tinkerin' builders"

tinker is a discord bot that builds software with groups of people. someone starts a session, everyone pitches ideas, the group votes, and the bot codes the winner live. the whole thing runs on bitcoin lightning — anyone can top up credits to keep it going.

how it works

IDLE — hanging out, answering questions, vibing
↓
IDEATION — everyone drops ideas. 2 minutes on the clock.
↓
SYNTHESIS — ideas get combined into 3 proposals. group votes.
↓
BUILD — winning idea gets built live. code flies.
↓
ITERATE — group gives feedback, bot builds the next round.
↓
WRAP — summary of what got built and who helped.

funded by lightning

inference costs are paid through ppq.ai with bitcoin lightning micropayments. no accounts, no credit cards. anyone can zap sats to keep the bot running.
